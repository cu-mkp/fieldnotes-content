In order to facilitate the consolidation of the research completed under the Making and Knowing Project it is vital that all information uploaded to the wiki is catalogued and identified correctly. The responsibility for tagging and identifying research lies with the individual researcher. No one will be better informed about your research than you will, so take the time to identify it. When uploading information to the wiki, every item (i.e. pages, media, files) must be tagged and named correctly. Doing so will greatly aid in consolidating this information for the critical edition and preserve the integrity of the research the students complete. The directions below describe the established procedures that each researcher is required to follow.

If you have any questions on the below standards and/or are not sure about how to proceed, please contact Naomi Rosenkranz.

Note:
  • Images and videos are housed in the Project Flickr.
  • Annotations are housed in the Project Google Drive.
