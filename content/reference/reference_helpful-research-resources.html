A number of further resources to consult:
  • Student Files - Reference Folder, including:
    • Examples, references, sources, bibliographies
    • Information and research (on the manuscript or its contents more generally)
    • Materials info/research (Project research and information on specific materials, such as blue pigments, lake pigments, armor, oils, solvents)
    • Scientific info docs (characterization techniques, etc)
    • Speaker presentations from the offerings of HIST GR8906
  • Technique Videos - a number of "making" videos with information about techniques, process, materials, etc.
  • Painter's Manual - 2016-17 Project research into BnF Ms. Fr. 640 as possible painter's manual, including categorization of colormaking recipes within the manuscript.
  • Pigment resources
  • Pigment glossary
  • COLOUR ConTEXT database (it’s a slightly confusing interface, so look for the “search” bar in upper left corner and enter your term there).
  • Technical Art History Website, University of Delaware
  • CAMEO: Conservation & Art Materials Encyclopedia Online (explore, and check “Materials database”)
  • Related Casting Recipes - further recipes and information about casting techniques in the manuscript.
