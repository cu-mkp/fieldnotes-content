Subject: Madder Red Lake 1

  • Madder: root must make into powder
    • Boil in K2CO3 --> solution dye
    • Pigment: particles through precipitation with potash alum
    • Cloth as substrate for pigment
  • Lake: from lac --> deep red pigment
  • Starting material: heterogeneous mix of twig-like and red/orange fragments
  • After grinding: COARSE orange powder, some variance in powder/chunk sizes
  • Couldn't transfer all over because powder was very fine --> H2O volume doesn't need to be exact
  • Soak in H2O --> H2O turns yellow-ish

Subject: Painting Madder Lake
  • Nice vein-like patterns in the filter paper
  • Very brittle from the paper but comes off very powder like with some chunks
  • Mix with egg yolk
  • Egg will dry very fast and hard, doesn't react to light --> will stiffen fast, small little strokes/cross hatching necessary; lasts longer
  • As painting: sticky, so as it dries it gets harder to paint as the brush sticks more to the square
  • Before yolk: brick powder like; after: BBQ sauce like color; added some water later on to make less chunky
  • Tried painting another layer in the other direction: more definition to layer
