Name: Teresa Soley and Njeri Ndungu
Date and Time: 2016. 2. 22, 11:00am
Location: Chandler 206
Subject: Flower Still-LIfe

Applied powdered burnt sienna + cold-pressed linseed oil mixture (pigment) to canvases sized with rabbit-skin glue.


We mixed powdered burt sienna pigment with linseed oil, gradually adding more of each to achieve a thick paste. We mixed the oil and pigment with a pallet knife, scraping edged periodically. We also mulled the mixture to achieve a smoother and more integrated paste.

We applied the paint to the already sized canvas surface using a pallet knife, scraping the surface to ensure a smooth and even application.


The pained canvas was placed upright in the fume hood to dry.
