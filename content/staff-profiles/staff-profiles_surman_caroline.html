Caroline Surman
Project Assistant to the Making and Knowing Project and Center for Science and Society
cms2274@columbia.edu

I graduated from Barnard College in May 2016 with a major in Anthropology and a minor in Environmental Science. I conducted qualitative and quantitative research in New York, Denmark, Sweden, and Turkey. I researched the art/craft dichotomy in New York City and submitted my senior thesis on interfaith organizations in a post 9/11 state. In my spare time, I have been a ceramicist for over 10 years and was a coordinator at the Barnard Clay Collective, the student-run ceramic studio for the Columbia community, for over 3 years.

Currently I work with both the Making and Knowing Project and the Center for Science and Society.
