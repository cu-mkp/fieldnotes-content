Charles Kang
cdk2118@columbia.edu
Ph.D. Candidate, Department of Art History and Archaeology, Columbia University
2016-17 Interpretive Fellow, The Frick Collection

Charles specializes in eighteenth-century French visual culture, with a particular focus on intersections between art and science. His project, Before the Reality Effect , explores the use of wax by artists and anatomists in eighteenth-century France. He holds an MA in art history from Williams College/Clark Art Institute.
