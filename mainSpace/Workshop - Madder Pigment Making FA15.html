Original Recipe:
Ingredients:
  • 2g coarsely ground madder root
  • Polyester netting bag (or cheesecloth or something similar)
  • 60 ml water
  • 1g potash alum
  • 0.4g potash
  • 40 ml water
  • Water to wash
