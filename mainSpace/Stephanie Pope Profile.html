Bio
Stephanie is a first-year PhD student in the English department at Princeton University, and her research interests lie in the field of early modern intellectual history and material culture. Prior to moving to Princeton, Stephanie obtained her undergraduate degree and Master's from the University of Oxford; during her time there she worked on projects including an investigation into the composition and provenance of a seventeenth-century English grimoire, and a study of the Renaissance fascination with the Egyptian hieroglyph. Outside of work, Stephanie is a gymnast and dancer, a keen traveller, and a fervently enthusiastic (if abysmal) karaoke singer.
