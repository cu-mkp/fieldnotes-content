Subject: Making my verdigris

Ingredients:

  • Glass jar and lid (obtained from class)

  • 1 copper sheet

  • Distilled white vinegar with 5% acidity

  • Cotton string

Procedure:

I poured an unmeasured amount of vinegar to the jar.

I tied the copper sheet with the string and hung it over the vinegar so that it would not touch the vinegar.

I closed the lid.

I placed the jar in a dark and far cupboard.

I warned my household members not to touch the jar.
