I received my the materials to make my verdigris -- copper, string, and a mason jar -- in class. After purchasing some vinegar at the store, I came home and set up my verdigris according to the instructions found in Merrifield, pg. 124, no. 125. I suspended the copper above the vinegar by tying the string around the strip of copper and positioning it at an angle above the copper in the jar. I secured the string by pinning it against the edge of the jar with the jar lid. I set it in a warm spot in my kitchen where I knew it would be undisturbed.
